Antique Craft

Documentary Short Film
Director Cinematography Editing

THE STORY

A documentary exploring the value of antique craftsmanship in a digital age. Featuring interviews, emotional storytelling, and a deep dive into clock and watch repair culture. The narrative highlights the patience, precision, and passion required to keep history alive.

Tools Used: Adobe Premiere Pro After Effects Sony A7III Macro Lens Zoom H6 Recorder

VISUAL STYLE

Macro Cinematography: To capture the intricate details of watch mechanisms, I utilized macro lenses to reveal a mechanical world unseen by the naked eye.

Atmosphere: Used warm, practical lighting to evoke a sense of nostalgia and intimacy, mirroring the artisan's workshop environment.

EDITING & PACING

  • Rhythmic Editing: The cuts were timed to the ticking of clocks and the rhythmic sounds of tools, creating a seamless audio-visual flow.
  • Narrative Structure: Intercut between the artisan's focused silence and his passionate narration to balance technical process with emotional depth.

Emotional Nightlight

Physical Computing & Prototyping
Emotion Design Story Telling

THE CONCEPT

A nightlight prototype designed to convey the emotion of peace and calmness. Encased in a transparent sphere, the design features a green forest landscape illuminated by soft beams of light. It adds aesthetic comfort to a living space while acting as a physical embodiment of stillness, balance, and integrity.

Materials: Transparent Sphere Leaf LED Lights Wool Glue

FROM ANXIETY TO CALM

Initial Idea: Originally, I planned a chaotic, enclosed space using wool shading and a clock base to express anxiety.

The Pivot: I shifted direction to create a "beautiful atmosphere" that calms people down. I fused Leaf LED lights with nature elements to radiate specific emotions.

PROBLEM SOLVING

  • Challenge: The Leaf LED lights were initially too harsh and bright to convey the intended softness.
  • Solution: I used twisted wool to warp around the lights. This diffused the brightness, blocked excess light, and added a "cozy" layered texture to the visuals.
