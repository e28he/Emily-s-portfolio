Graphic Design

Visual communication exploring typography, layout, and creative narratives.

01

Creative Identity

Self-Expression | AI & Mixed Media
Theme: Fragmented Memories

This series serves as a visual manifesto of my identity as a designer. Using the concept of "collage," I pieced together different aspects of my creative journey, reflecting themes of growth, healing, and innovation.

Generative AI Integration

I experimented with Adobe Firefly (Beta AI) to generate unique textures and surreal elements. By blending these AI-generated assets with traditional vector graphics in Illustrator, I explored the intersection of human creativity and machine intelligence.

Design Philosophy

As displayed in the typography poster, I believe design should be "Good, Clean, Emotional, and Innovative." This project visualizes these core values through bold typography and layered compositions.

02

Poster Design

Advertising Narrative | Parks & Rec
The Narrative: Urban Oasis

This diptych creates a visual dialogue between Action and Calm. The left poster ("Explore nature today") uses bold vertical text to urge immediate action, while the right poster ("Find your peace") offers an escape from the noisy city.

Color Grading Strategy

The Challenge: Unifying two photos with different original tones (Winter vs. Fall).

The Solution: Using Photoshop filters, I adjusted both images to a warm Orange/Fall Palette. This unified tone communicates warmth and comfort, enhancing the emotional appeal.

Typography & Effects

I utilized opacity and blur effects in Illustrator to create a "reflective" atmosphere. The hierarchy is strictly managed through font weight, guiding the eye naturally to the QR codes.

Adobe Photoshop Adobe Illustrator Filter Effects
A visual narrative inviting audiences to find peace outdoors.
03

Magazine Layout

Editorial Design | Nature & Growth
The Metaphor: Nature & Growth

The magazine's theme parallels my own journey in the design field: transitioning from a beginner to a skilled creator. Just as nature grows organically, my understanding of composition and hierarchy evolved throughout this project.

From Intuition to Structure

Previously, my designs were intuitive but often cluttered. Learning Adobe InDesign was a turning point. I utilized modular grids and alignment tools to bring precision to the layout.

Design Philosophy

This project shifted my focus from "what looks pretty" to User-Centered Design. I applied principles of simplicity and physical alignment to align the design with the user's expectations.

Adobe InDesign Grid Systems Typography
The Cover: Symbolizing the threshold of a new career path.
Grid System: Using alignment to organize dense text.

Trifold Brochure

Travel Design | Print Production
Information Architecture

The challenge was organizing dense travel information (schedules, maps, tips) into a compact format. I prioritized hierarchy—using bold headings and color coding to guide the reader's eye.

Visual Identity

A fresh, modern style that integrates the color scheme with the content structure, ensuring a seamless reading experience for travelers on the go.
