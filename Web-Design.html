KONECT APP

Mobile UX/UI | Product Design | 2024
The Challenge

Students often struggle to find accessible contacts and feel intimidated to reach out to professionals. Existing platforms like LinkedIn can be overwhelming for beginners.

The Solution

KONECT facilitates mentorship through event recommendations and direct invitations. It creates a "safe space" for students to build their professional network with confidence.

TARGET AUDIENCE: PERSONAS

Alice (23) Co-op Developer / Student

Goal: Expand network but scared to "bother" seniors.
Frustration: Doesn't know how to start a conversation on LinkedIn.

Nimish (18) First-year Math Student

Goal: Secure internships but has limited connections.
Frustration: Finds professionals "intimidating".

IDEATION: FROM BRAINSTORM TO CONCEPT

We explored group brainstorming ideas before converging on specific layout concepts.

Phase 1: Group Brainstorming
Initial team brainstorm session to explore various features and user flows.
Phase 2: Layout Concept A
Structuring the information architecture with a focus on vertical scrolling events.
Phase 3: Layout Concept B
Alternative approach prioritizing a dashboard view for immediate engagement.

MEMENTOS

BET 350 | Product Strategy & User Journey | 2024
The Problem

Users struggle to efficiently find and organize past travel photos. The experience of digging through cluttered camera rolls is frustrating, making it hard to relive meaningful memories or share them meaningfully.

The Solution

Mementos is a digital scrapbook that offers Location-based sorting for quick search, AI activity suggestions to help planning, and auto-generated albums to facilitate reflection and sharing.
