The Concept: This logo emphasizes a combination of love, adventure, and creativity. It is a visual storytelling piece that represents meaningful connections and a passion for exploring the world.

The Solution: By combining multiple symbols into a cohesive form, the design captures the client's adventurous spirit while maintaining balance and emphasis.

Design Process & Iteration

From Light Bulb to Camera... and Back

Initially, I designed a simple light bulb. However, to better capture the client's worldview, I pivoted to redesigning the logo as a camera. I thought this would better represent "viewing the world with love."

The Pivot: Despite the concept, the camera design became too complicated and messy. It lost the clean impact I wanted. I made the strategic decision to return to the light bulb concept, which allowed for a simpler, more well-combined composition that successfully integrated the mountains and chef hat elements.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator (Pen Tool for tracing, Direct Selection for smoothing anchors, Live Paint Bucket for coloring).

Color Theory: I chose Blue and Green to represent the mountain and nature elements. Combined, they make the light bulb resemble a globe, further satisfying the client's adventurous spirit. The red heart provides contrast and emphasis.
